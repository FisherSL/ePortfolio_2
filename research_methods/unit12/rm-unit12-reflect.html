Reflecting on Unit 12

In Unit 12, we focused on project management and managing risk within a project. Effective project management involves controlling processes and activities related to a project, particularly within computing infrastructure and information systems. We explored various project management methodologies and learned that a ‘one size fits all’ approach does not work due to the diverse environments and objectives of different projects.

We also discussed the importance of selecting appropriate metrics to measure project performance and assessing risks, acknowledging that no project is risk-free. Risk management involves identifying and planning for significant potential risks, which often are not visible at the start of a project. We learned how to manage changes during a project to minimise their impact on project scope, cost, and timeline while taking advantage of new opportunities.

Key Takeaways:

  1. Project Management: Understanding the concept and importance of project management, including the project life cycle and methodologies.
  2. Risk Management: Learning how to assess, plan, and manage risks and changes within a project to maintain quality and control costs.
  3. Change Management: Implementing a change control process to handle changes effectively during a project.
  4. Remote Collaboration: Appreciating the technologies and software needed to support remote project collaboration.

Summary of Activities

  • Lecturecast: Participated in a lecturecast on project management and risk management.
  • Self-Test Quiz: Completed a quiz to reinforce understanding of the unit concepts.
  • Portfolio Submission: Prepared and submitted the final e-Portfolio, reflecting on learning and professional development.

Throughout the course, I found the e-Portfolio to be a valuable tool for tracking my learning and professional development. However, the lack of consistency between modules regarding the e-Portfolio submission was frustrating. More frequent and consistent emphasis on the e-Portfolio across modules would have been helpful. As someone balancing work and study, managing the numerous tasks was challenging, and I believe a more balanced workload would improve the learning experience.

So what?

This unit highlighted the importance of effective project and risk management in ensuring project success. The activities and assessments helped me develop a comprehensive understanding of managing projects and handling risks, which will be invaluable in my professional practice. The research proposal and literature review aspects were extremely helpful for future studies.

What next?

Moving forward, I will apply the project management and risk management techniques learned in this unit to my professional projects. I will continue to refine my e-Portfolio to showcase my skills and development. Additionally, I will advocate for more balanced workloads and consistent e-Portfolio integration across modules to enhance the learning experience for future students.
