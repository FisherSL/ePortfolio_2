Reflecting on Unit 9

Overall, I found the statistical worksheets useful as I had never used Excel to perform a t-test and as previously stated, the opportunity to interpret statistical findings will stand me in good stead for the capstone project. Unfortunately, there was a great deal of frustration in finding the Excel files for the examples and exercises.

After raising the issue, apparently this module has undergone a recent ‘refresh’ which resulted in the issues experienced. I found the whole experience frustrating and unnecessarily time consuming since the worksheets are a compulsory part of the eportfolio.
