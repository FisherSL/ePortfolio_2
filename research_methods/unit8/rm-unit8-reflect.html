Reflecting on Unit 8

Unit 8 shifted the focus from research methods to data analysis and visualisation. This unit highlighted the differences between handling quantitative and qualitative data and explored various ways to analyse and present them effectively.

Qualitative data, made up of observations, images, and words, is more subjective and open to interpretation, making it harder to remain unbiased. Coding responses and using software for efficient analysis are crucial steps. On the other hand, quantitative data analysis involves clear stages of data preparation and analysis.

Data visualisation is an important aspect of data presentation. Dashboards, as suggested by Microsoft (2023), help in organising and displaying important information at a glance, providing actionable insights for businesses.

Key Takeaways:

  1. Qualitative Data Analysis: Understanding the coding and categorisation of qualitative data for better interpretation.
  2. Quantitative Data Analysis: Recognising the clear distinction between data preparation and analysis stages.
  3. Data Visualisation: Learning the importance of dashboards and various charts to present data effectively.

Summary of Activities

  • Solo Reading: Engaged in individual reading to understand different types of data analysis and visualisation techniques.
  • Worksheets: Completed worksheets related to data inference and chart creation using Excel, reinforcing practical skills.
  • Research Proposal Outline: Missed the opportunity to submit a research proposal outline due to time constraints, but managed to keep up with other activities.
  • Seminar Preparation: Prepared for the seminar by working on charts worksheets in Excel, enhancing my understanding of data inference.
  • Seminar Recording: Watched the seminar recording, which was crucial for grasping the concepts of data inference and visualisation.

Unit 8 provided valuable insights into data analysis and visualisation. Although I missed some opportunities due to time constraints, the worksheets and seminar recordings were beneficial in strengthening my understanding.

So what?

The unit emphasised the importance of effectively analysing and presenting both qualitative and quantitative data. Balancing multiple tasks remains a challenge, but engaging with seminar recordings and practical worksheets helped in understanding key concepts.

What next?

Going forward, I aim to refine my skills in data analysis and visualisation. I will focus on managing my time more effectively to ensure I can participate fully in all activities. The insights gained from this unit will be crucial for my future research projects, especially in presenting data clearly and effectively.
