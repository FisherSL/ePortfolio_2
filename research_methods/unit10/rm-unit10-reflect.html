Unit 10: Reflections

Most time this week was spent finalising the unit 10 summative assessment which was a research proposal presentation and transcript. Whilst a challenge and requiring far more time than indicated in the module, this was a valuable exercise since a similar task will be required for the capstone. This was the first time I had extensively used a reference manager and AI tools to help with my research and I will certainly be using them in the future. I also felt that I may have turned a corner with critical writing although I await tutor feedback to see if this is the case!

UPDATE: Feedback regarding criticality from the tutor was positive and graded outstanding.
