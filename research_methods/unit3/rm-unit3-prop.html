e-Portfolio Activity: Research Proposal Review

Considering My Project: "Unveiling Digital Cults: The Influence of AI and Social Media on Mass Movements"

For my project on the influence of AI and social media on mass movements, I have carefully selected research methods and data collection techniques that align with my objectives and the complexity of the topic.

Which Methods Would Suit My Purpose?

From Dawson (2015), I found that certain research methods align well with my project goals:

Exploratory Studies

  • Rationale: Since my project involves exploring the relatively new and complex phenomenon of digital cults, an exploratory study is most appropriate. This method will help me understand what is happening, seek new insights, and assess phenomena from a fresh perspective.
  • Application: I will start with literature searches to gather existing knowledge on digital cults, AI, and social media. Following this, I plan to use open-ended questionnaires and conduct interviews to gather qualitative data from individuals involved in or affected by digital cults.

Descriptive Studies

  • Rationale: Descriptive studies will allow me to review and evaluate existing theories and knowledge, as well as describe the characteristics of digital cults and the role of AI and social media in their formation and growth.
  • Application: I will use case studies to describe specific instances of digital cults, detailing their development, the influence of AI algorithms, and social media dynamics.

Which Data Collection Methods Would I Consider Using?

Based on Dawson (2015) and other readings, I will use the following data collection methods:

Surveys

  • Rationale: Surveys are effective for collecting a large amount of data from a sizable population in an economical way. They will help me understand the broader impact of digital cults.
  • Application: I plan to distribute online surveys to gather data on public perceptions and experiences with digital cults and the influence of AI and social media on these movements.

Interviews

  • Rationale: Interviews provide in-depth qualitative insights and allow for a deeper understanding of individual experiences and expert opinions.
  • Application: I will conduct semi-structured interviews with experts in AI, social media analysts, and former members of digital cults to gather detailed qualitative data.

Case Studies

  • Rationale: Case studies allow for an in-depth exploration of specific digital cults to understand their formation, dynamics, and impact.
  • Application: I will perform case studies on well-documented digital cults, using a combination of interviews, social media analysis, and document reviews to provide a comprehensive understanding.

Content Analysis

  • Rationale: Content analysis is useful for analysing the content and communication patterns within digital cults.
  • Application: I will utilise tools for content analysis to examine social media posts, focusing on language patterns, key themes, and the spread of information within these groups.

Which Required Skills Will I Need to Have or Develop for the Chosen Project?

Based on the readings, I will need to develop several key skills:

Literature Review Skills

  • Application: I need to be able to search, review, and synthesise existing literature on digital cults, AI, and social media.

Survey Design and Analysis

  • Application: I will craft effective survey questions, distribute surveys online, and analyse the results using statistical software.

Interviewing Skills

  • Application: I will need to conduct both structured and unstructured interviews, develop questions, practice active listening, and perform qualitative data analysis.

Data Analysis Skills

  • Application: I will analyse qualitative data from interviews and case studies, as well as quantitative data from surveys. This includes familiarity with tools like NVivo for qualitative data analysis and statistical software for quantitative analysis.

Content Analysis Skills

  • Application: I will use content analysis software to analyse large datasets from social media and understand natural language processing (NLP) techniques to assess the content of social media posts and other digital communications.

Critical Thinking and Analytical Skills

  • Application: I will need to evaluate and interpret data critically to draw meaningful conclusions and ensure the reliability and validity of my findings.

By combining these methods and data collection techniques, I am confident that I will be able to comprehensively explore the influence of AI and social media on mass movements and provide valuable insights.

References
