Reflecting on Unit 7

Unit 7 explored quantitative methods, focusing on inferential statistics and hypothesis testing. This unit provided insights into using statistical inference to draw conclusions about populations from sample data, along with the basics of probability and hypothesis testing.

Inferential statistics aims to derive patterns and properties about a larger group by studying a smaller sample. This involves extracting meaningful insights from data while acknowledging uncertainty. Hypothesis testing emerged as a method to evaluate assumptions about population values based on sample data.

Key Takeaways:

  1. Inferential Statistics: Understanding how to draw conclusions about populations from sample data.
  2. Probability: Recognising the role of probability in quantifying uncertainty.
  3. Hypothesis Testing: Learning to assess assumptions about population values through hypothesis testing.

Summary of Activities

  • Lecturecast: Engaged in individual learning through lecturecasts on summary measures and inference, gaining insights into inferential statistics and hypothesis testing.
  • Collaborative Discussion: Participated in a discussion on the accuracy of information, exploring challenges in balancing workload with part-time studies and full-time work.
  • Excel Worksheets: Completed a hypothesis testing worksheet using Excel, reinforcing learning from lecturecasts.
  • Seminar: Watched a seminar on hypothesis testing, gaining insights into new methodologies applicable to research projects.
  • Literature Review Submission: Submitted a literature review, managing workload alongside other tasks was challenging.

Unit 7 provided valuable insights into inferential statistics and hypothesis testing. However, managing workload alongside other commitments was challenging. Reflecting on the experience, adjustments may be necessary to ensure a balanced workload and optimal learning outcomes.

So what?

The workload intensity of Unit 7 posed challenges in managing multiple tasks within a single week, impacting engagement with collaborative discussions and allocation of time to the literature review. Balancing workload with part-time studies and full-time work remains a key consideration for future units.

What next?

Building on the knowledge gained in Unit 7, I aim to further refine my understanding of inferential statistics and hypothesis testing, applying these techniques to future research projects. Additionally, I will seek strategies to manage workload more effectively and prioritise tasks to ensure optimal learning outcomes, although I do think this needs to be refined by the course leaders too.
